About Me V

I'm a Front End web developer based in London.

I love developing, producing music and being creative.

I grew up in Brighton, did a Music Tech degree at London College of Music and studied Web Development at a General Assembly bootcamp course.

I love creating beautifully styled, useful projects from start to finish and try to combine my passions of music and developing as much as possible.
