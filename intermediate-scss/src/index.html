About

I'm a Full Stack web developer based in London.

I love developing, producing music and being creative. I grew up in Brighton, did a Music Tech degree at London College of Music and studied Web Development at a General Assembly bootcamp course.

I love creating beautifully styled, engaging projects from start to finish and try to combine my passions of music and developing as much as possible.

General Assembly

In the summer of 2017 I joined a General Assembly Web development immersive course.

The GA immersive opened my eyes to the world of coding and has shown me how creative and exciting being a developer can be.

GA showed me that I not only had the ability to become a developer, but that I could also have a lot of fun doing it.

Javascript Game

An audio visual memory game

Soundboard

A multiplayer audio visual memory game. To play, you must repeat back the computer's sequence by hitting the keys 1-8 on your keyboard. Get the sequence right to progress to the next round, fail and enter the sudden death round. The difficulty increases with each round until a player faulters and there is a winner.

Mix Tape

A song suggestion app for 'YouTube DJs'. Once the user inputs a song the app will return a suggestion which is not only from a related artist but also rhythmically matched to the input. The app can then stack the songs in video players, allowing users to create video playlists.

Ecommerce App

Wabi Sabi

An Ecommerce site for people to buy and sell homemade products. Buyers can add items to their shopping basket, enter card details at the checkout. Sellers can receive orders and mark them as shipped. I was responsible for the orders functionality which sends orders to the relevant sellers and allows them to update their status.

Spotlight

A discussion forum for social issues. Users can post articles, comment and tag each other in the discussion for cohesive conversation. Once tagged, a user receives a notification in their nav bar and is directed to the article they were tagged in. My second group project, I took on a large part of the notifications functionality and styling tasks.
